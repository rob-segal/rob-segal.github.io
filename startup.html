Background Exoskeletons

I had been working on an exoskeleton for hand rehabilitation with some colleagues. It wasn't getting traction in hospitals, so we decided to start a business to bring exoskeletons to VR. Exoflex

Virtual Reality Problem

Unfortunately, we did not have enough funds to develop the technology immediately, so we looked at problems in the VR industry that needed solutions. Motion sickness was a huge problem, where users had the mismatch of virtually moving, but being physically in the same spot.

Alto Hardware Solution

We developed the Alto to move around virtual reality, while also physically moving in reality. I developed the prototype of our first product, a VR “hoverboard” with haptic feedback to alleviate motion sickness. Alto
