Award-Winning Projects

I was a key contributor to these projects, all of which won Good Design Awards

Thermal Armour

A blanket that regulates body temperature to prevent hypothermia before, during and after surgery.
Good Design Award Gold Winner 2024.
I developed firmware and designed a temperature control algorithm.
https://good-design.org/projects/thermal-armour-modular-warming-system/

Bucklemeup

A wireless seatbelt safety system.
Good Design Award Winner 2023.
I developed firmware and Bluetooth communications.
https://good-design.org/projects/bucklemeup/

EO Swimbetter

A wearable device to track swimming technique.
Good Design Award Winner 2022.
I developed firmware, motion tracking and storage software, as well as Bluetooth communications.
https://good-design.org/projects/eo-swimbetter/

SureVis

A thermal imaging device designed to monitor elevated body temperatures.
Good Design Award Winner 2020.
I developed software for controlling the hardware, a temperature monitoring algorithm, and the graphical user interface, within weeks following COVID lockdowns.
https://good-design.org/projects/surevis/
